About

I am an MSc student in Infection Biology with a background in Biomedical Sciences. My work focuses on transforming complex literature into structured, decision-useful scientific outputs for academic and biomedical contexts. My areas of interest include host–pathogen interactions, antimicrobial resistance, microbiome research, and infection biology.

# Scientific Curation Portfolio

This repository contains my public portfolio website for structured scientific and biomedical evidence curation.

The portfolio features selected samples of curated literature outputs designed for academic and biomedical contexts, with a focus on evidence extraction, mechanistic synthesis, and multi-paper evidence mapping.

## What this portfolio includes

Examples of work presented in this portfolio include:

- Article Evidence Extraction
- Mechanism Reports
- Multi-paper Evidence Maps
- Structured Evidence Dossiers
- Scientific Landscape Briefs
- Methodological Appraisal Briefs

## Areas of focus

My main areas of interest include:

- Infection biology
- Host–pathogen interactions
- Antimicrobial resistance
- Microbiome research
- Biomedical evidence synthesis
